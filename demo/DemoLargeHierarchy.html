Large Hierarchy Navigation Demo

In order to easy browse and navigate large hierarchy, chart folds as many items as needed in order to fit it into available screen space. Only current cursor item and its immediate children and its parents are shown in normal form. Depending on user options chart displays collection of user selected items and selection path items up to the root item. All other items displayed depending on available space.
